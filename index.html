Our Classes


Hatha Yoga

Gentle, slow-paced practice focusing on basic postures, breathing techniques, and meditation. Perfect for beginners and those seeking relaxation.

Vinyasa Flow

Dynamic sequences linking breath with movement. Build strength, flexibility, and endurance while flowing through creative poses.

Restorative Yoga

Deep relaxation practice using props to support the body in passive poses. Ideal for stress relief and healing.

Yin Yoga

Meditative practice with long-held poses targeting deep connective tissues. Cultivate patience, stillness, and inner awareness.

Hot Yoga

Challenging practice in a heated room to increase flexibility, detoxification, and mental focus through intense postures.

Meditation

Guided mindfulness sessions to develop concentration, reduce stress, and cultivate inner peace through various meditation techniques.

Yoga Retreats


Mountain Retreat

Escape to the serene Himalayas for a 7-day transformative journey. Daily yoga, meditation, and nature immersion in breathtaking landscapes.

Beach Sanctuary

Connect with the ocean's rhythm in our 5-day coastal retreat. Sunrise yoga, beach meditation, and rejuvenating spa treatments.

Wellness Weekend

Short but intensive 3-day retreat focused on detox, mindfulness, and establishing a sustainable home practice routine.
