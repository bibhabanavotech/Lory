About Me

I'm Lorey Pro, and I've dedicated over 30 years to helping people achieve their fitness and performance goals. With a Master's degree in Kinesiology & Recreation and a lifelong passion for athletics, I've worked across commercial, private, public, non-profit, and university settings as a coach, trainer, and health professional. I've also competed in endurance events from 5Ks to 50-mile races and triathlons up to the half-ironman distance. Most importantly, I've had the privilege of helping countless people live healthier, stronger, and more balanced lives.

Why Fitness Matters

I believe physical fitness is one of the most powerful tools we have for living a longer, healthier, and more fulfilling life. Exercise goes far beyond weight loss or building muscle — it strengthens the heart, improves energy, sharpens the mind, and reduces the risk of chronic disease. Just as importantly, it builds confidence, resilience, and a sense of balance in daily life.

Movement is medicine. Whether it's walking, running, cycling, strength training, or group fitness, consistent exercise can transform the way you feel and function. My goal is to help people discover the kind of movement that excites them, fits their lifestyle, and supports their long-term health.
